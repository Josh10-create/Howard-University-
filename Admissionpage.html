Admission Requirements

Interested Candidates must:

  • be 16 years and above
  • score at least 200 at the recent Jamb Examination
  • have 5 credit pass in their Senior School Certificate Examination (SSCE)
  • provide birth certificate to confirm age
  • provide JAMB Result
  • provide O'Level Result
  • provide 8 passports for indentification purposes
  • Provide JAMB Registration Slip
  • and others ask by the Admission Officer
