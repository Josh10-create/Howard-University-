Welcome Message from University Vice Chancellor

"Dear students, parents, faculty, and staff,

It is my great pleasure to welcome you to Howard University, a vibrant community of scholars dedicated to academic excellence, innovation, and social responsibility.

As we embark on a new academic year, I want to emphasize the importance of collaboration, creativity, and critical thinking in our pursuit of knowledge and understanding. Our university is committed to providing a supportive and inclusive environment that fosters growth, discovery, and success.

I encourage you to take advantage of the many opportunities available to you, from academic programs and research initiatives to extracurricular activities and community engagement. Your time at Howard University is a chance to explore your passions, develop your talents, and make lifelong connections.

I look forward to seeing the amazing things you will achieve and contributing to our university's mission to shape the leaders of tomorrow.

Welcome to Howard University!

Sincerely,
Ben Vince III -- Vice Chancellor
